Melt In Your Mouth Chicken

Ingredients:

  1. Boneless Chicken Breasts
  2. Sour Cream
  3. Parmesan Cheese
  4. Pepper
  5. Seasoned Salt
  6. Garlic Powder
Let's Get to Cooking!

Preheat over to 375. Coat a 9x13 baking dish with PAM and set aside. In a medium bowl combine the sour cream, garlic powder, seasoned salt, pepper and 1 cup of Parmesan cheese. Lay the chicken breasts into the prepared pan in a single line, then spread the MIYM mix on top and sprinkle with more Parmesan cheese. Bake for 25-30 minutes. Serve and Enjoy!

Amish Broccoli Salad

  • 1 head of broccoli, chopped
  • 1 head of cauliflower, chopped
  • 1 cup of mayonnaise(lite if you want)
  • 1 cup of sour cream(lite if you want)
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2lb of bacon, fried and crumbled
  • 1 cup of shredded Cheddar cheese(fat-free if you want)

Mix all together, chill and enjoy!
